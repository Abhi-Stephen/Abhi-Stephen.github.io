Roasted Turkey with Herb Stuffing

Ingredients:

  • 12-pound turkey
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh herbs (sage, rosemary, thyme)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (165°C).
  2. Rinse and pat dry the turkey.
  3. Mix the softened butter and herbs. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Spread the herb butter under the turkey's skin and over the top.

Total Cooking Time: 210 minutes
